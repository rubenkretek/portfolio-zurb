Jobs

A built-in jobs board that can be easily managed by FirstCol staff

FirstCol needed their old website to be rebuilt in WordPress, with the addition of the ability to add and manage new jobs on offer.

I used an existing plugin as a base for the site’s functionality, and customised it to fit FirstCol’s needs.

Applications overhaul

New streamlined application process

Previously FirstCol took applications through an online form and then called applicants to tell them about available jobs.

I was able to cut out this step by allowing users to view available jobs before applying.

UX Review

I designed the application form to follow a logical layout, and added a progress bar and clear instructions alongside each section.

Time is money

FirstCol were experiencing a high rate of drop-offs with their previous form, as users were frustrated by the length of it.

To maximise leads, I added an alternative ‘fast-track’ form, which is a stripped back version of the main application form that takes 5 minutes to complete. This allows FirstCol to still make contact with the potential candidate who would otherwise have dropped off.

Bespoke WordPress theme

FirstCol supplied a fresh branding guideline that I used when building their new site.

This posed many challenges which couldn’t be solved with an off-the-shelf theme, so I built a custom one.

All job modules on the site needed to appear in a specific way that wasn’t present in the plugin or any other theme. I used a combination of PHP, CSS and JS to achieve the required layout.

Responsive

A substantial proportion of FirstCol’s traffic came through mobile devices, but their old site was not well optimised for smaller screens.

When desigining and building the website I tested the UX across many device types to ensure it functioned effectively.

Workshops

I worked closely with FirstCol from the beginning to ensure they would be able to use what I built for them.

Towards the end of the project I led a workshop at their offices to teach them how to use their new system.

Finished Product

A system that streamlined the client’s application process.
